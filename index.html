About Me


I'm a Software Engineering at Samsung R&D Institute India - Bangalore. I joined in June 2016, and I work in the health and fitness research team of the Advanced Technology Lab. I'm involved with non-invasive monitoring of everyday activities and vital paramters for health tracking of an individual. My work involves working with sensors which are intergated into wearables/smartphones, or can be integrated in the future.

Prior to joining Samsung, I finished my B.Tech from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur, majoring in Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering.
